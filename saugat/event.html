SEPIA’S SOMMELIER SERIES

We’re fortunate enough to frequently host intimate dinners with world-class winemakers, including Virginia Wilcock of Vasse Felix and Vanessa Hart of Silver Oak. Held in our stylish, sexy event space, these events pair Michelin-starred cuisine with cutting-edge wines you won’t find anywhere in Chicago. Tickets are limited, ensuring a private, face-to-face experience with trailblazers in the wine industry—if you act fast. Visit our Tock page below for all the latest updates and ticket information.

" making pasta by hand is an act of love."

We’re excited to offer a variety of interactive pasta classes at Parcheggio! Led by Executive Chef Andrew Piccinin, these hands-on lessons bring together traditional pasta-making techniques with helpful tips that will have cooks of all levels ready to whip up a bowl handmade noodles. Since guests are sure to work up an appetite, each class will be followed by a three-course dinner. Wine and cocktails will also be available for purchase onsite.!
