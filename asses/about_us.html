~About us~

Chick fil-a restaurant brings classic Italian dining to downtown Toronto in collaboration with our partner Chef Oliver Glowig, one of Rome's most celebrated chefs who has been awarded numerous Michelin Stars over his career. Experience locally sourced, seasonal ingredients through homemade pastas, fresh seafood and traditional desserts. Complement your dinner with aged selections from the cheese cave. The restaurant experience is elevated with wine dinners, cooking classes, tastings, workshops and other culinary opportunities.

Community

Maialino is proud to be located in the historic Gramercy Park neighborhood. We support a number of local community organizations, The Hole in the Wall Gang Camp, as well as hunger relief organizations like City Harvest, Share Our Strength, and the Bed-Stuy Campaign Against Hunger.
